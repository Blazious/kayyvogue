About The Artist

Hello, I'm Khaula

I'm a self-taught artist and nature enthusiast who finds inspiration in the world around me — from the delicate curve of a leaf to the golden light of a sunset. I’ve always been drawn to storytelling through art, and photography has become one of my favorite ways to freeze those fleeting moments of natural beauty.

Alongside my creative journey, I'm pursuing a degree in International Relations at the University of Nairobi. I believe understanding people and cultures across borders deeply enriches both my worldview and my art.

Painting, reading, and wandering with a camera in hand are my daily rituals. Whether it’s a brushstroke on canvas or a photo snapped in passing, each piece is a reflection of how I see and feel the world — quiet, colorful, and full of stories.

My art is shaped by emotion, curiosity, and a love for the ordinary. I work with acrylics, watercolors, and mixed media to bring my inner world to life — creating visuals that speak softly but leave a lasting impression.
